Behind the music

About Nik

With eight decades of experience in music, electronics, photography, writing, military service and life itself, Nik Boldrini brings a lifetime of stories to Absatively Music.

A longtime singer, guitarist and creative tinkerer, Nik uses modern tools as instruments for turning ideas into finished songs, artwork and videos. He calls the process Artful Intelligence: technology guided by human memory, humor, judgment and heart.

Absatively Music is his home for original songs, memorable instrumentals and the occasional idea that insists on becoming something—which he is unable to stop.
