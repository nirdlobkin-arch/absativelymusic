About Nik

With eight decades of experience, Nik Boldrini continues doing what he enjoys most—creating original music.

Through Absatively Music, he combines songwriting, musicianship, storytelling and what he calls Posilutely Artful Intelligence to produce songs and instrumentals that are thoughtful, entertaining and unmistakably his own.

Absatively Music is home to the occasional idea that insists on becoming something—which he is unable to stop.
